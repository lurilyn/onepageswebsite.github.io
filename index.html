COMPANY OVERVIEW

At Ideal Builder, we realize homebuilders have specific needs unique to the housing industry. You need a different sort of website. You need a different sort of marketing. You need something flexible, scalable, custom.

Industry-Based Expertise

Collectively, we have decades of experience in web design, Internet marketing, and SEO. We're experts by now, but we still like to think of ourselves as perpetual students--we're constantly learning, studying, testing, and implementing the latest and most effective online trends. All of our work is poured into one industry, one set of goals: yours.

Team Players

Partnership--that's what makes us the leading digital partner for homebuilders. We're not just programmers, and we're not just designers. We're analysts, consultants, and advertisers too. We're everything you need to start selling more houses.

Marketing Solutions

From there, we give you the option of handing the wheel over to us and letting us drive traffic to your site through inbound marketing. We'll run your Google Adwords campaign, your email blasts, your blogs, and even your daily social media.

One Stop Shop

Just as you don't expect your homebuyer to source all the right materials that go into building their dream home, we don't expect you to source multiple companies for all your online marketing needs. That's why we've built a diverse and experienced team dedicated to understanding homebuilders and your place in the digital age.

Here's How It Works

We start with a website, one that's designed to showcase your stunning homes and address your specific needs. It can be a simple solution for showcasing one particular community, or it can be a full-fledged company overview with interactive plat maps and custom design.
